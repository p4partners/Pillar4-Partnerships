Why our pricing
The shift from blue-link to AI-cited.
The old model
Performance-based & affiliate
Traditional affiliate and performance-based models assume the reader clicks a link, visits the brand's site, and converts there. Revenue flows back only when that full chain completes.

That model is breaking. AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity increasingly answer product questions directly, surfacing brand recommendations without the user ever clicking a link. Blue-link clicks to publisher sites are declining across search. The last-click attribution chain no longer tells the full story.
The P4 model
Hybrid: flat fee + commission
Our pricing reflects the real value of editorial placement in an AI-first world. A flat fee compensates for the influence we drive at every stage of the buying journey, including the growing share that happens before the click, inside an AI response, or in a reader's memory before they search at all.

Commission on top aligns our incentives with yours. We still have skin in the game on conversion. But the base fee ensures we're compensated for the full scope of brand influence we deliver, not just what last-click attribution can see.
The bottom line: Our content is already being cited in AI responses across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, driving brand awareness and purchase intent that never registers as a click. Flat-fee editorial partnerships are how you capture that value.
